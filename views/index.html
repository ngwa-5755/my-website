Welcome to the STEM Division Evaluation System

At PGCC, we strive to provide an efficient and streamlined evaluation process for our full-time faculty. Our system allows faculty members to submit self-assessment forms, supporting evidence, and receive valuable feedback from supervisors.

Features

Self-Assessment Forms

Easily submit your self-assessment forms online. Our system is designed to be user-friendly and efficient.

Supporting Evidence Upload

Upload supporting documents such as PDFs and images to complement your self-assessment.

Feedback and Review

Receive timely feedback and review from your supervisors to help you grow and improve in your role.

Get Started Today

Log in or register to access the evaluation system and start your self-assessment process.
